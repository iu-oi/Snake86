# snake86

A simple hungry snake game written in masm 8086 assembly language.

Author: iu_oi

---

***compile***

DOSBox 0.74 or any DOS you want

`masm snake86.asm`

`link snake86.obj`

`snake86.exe`

---

***how to play***

- Use WASD to steer your snake.
- 1 apple = 1 score = 1 body length
- Hitting the wall or your body is fatal.
